New here? Start here.

You don't have to have it figured out to walk through the door.

If you found Finding Eden in a hard moment — or if you're just curious — this page is designed for you. We'll walk through what this place is, what to expect, and where to go next. Take your time.

First thing

You don't have to be in crisis to come here.

Finding Eden is not a crisis line. It's not an emergency resource. It's a community healing space — which means it's for people in all different places in their journey.

You might be going through something hard right now. You might be recovering from something hard. You might just have that persistent feeling that something is off and you're not sure what.

All of that is welcome here.

"You don't need to perform your healing. You just need to show up."

What to expect

A space that meets you where you are.

🤝

No pressure to share

You can observe, listen, and participate at whatever level feels right. There's no requirement to open up on day one.

🎨

Multiple ways to engage

Movement, art, sound, writing, conversation — there are many pathways in. You don't have to use words to heal.

👥

A room full of real people

Everyone here is carrying something. That shared humanity is part of what makes it safe.

🌿

Non-clinical, non-judgmental

We are not therapists. We are humans building a space for honest community. Trained facilitators guide, not fix.

🕰️

Come and go as you need

There's no attendance requirement. Come when you can, take breaks when you need to, and return when you're ready.

💬

Anonymity is respected

In storytelling and reflection spaces, you can share anonymously. Nothing you share will be shared without your consent.

If you're in immediate crisis

Finding Eden is not a crisis resource. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health emergency, please reach out to:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency Services: Call 911
