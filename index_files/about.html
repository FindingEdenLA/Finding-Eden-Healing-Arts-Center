Get involved

Finding Eden is built one person at a time. Join us.

Whether you want to volunteer, collaborate as a facilitator or artist, donate, or simply stay connected — there's a place for you in this project.

How you can help

Many ways to be part of this.

🤸

Volunteer

Help with events — setup, facilitation support, documentation, guest welcome, and logistics.

🎨

Facilitate or Lead a Workshop

If you're a healer, artist, movement practitioner, or storyteller, we'd love to hear from you.

💚

Donate

Financial support funds venues, facilitators, art supplies, and the infrastructure to grow.

📸

Photograph or Document

Help capture the visual story of Finding Eden through photography or videography.

📣

Spread the Word

Share Finding Eden with people who might need it. Community growth starts with personal connection.

🤝

Partner or Sponsor

Businesses, foundations, and organizations can partner to support the vision at a larger scale.

Programs & workshops

Sample experiences that reflect the heart of Finding Eden.

These offerings can be hosted as stand-alone workshops, recurring classes, community circles, or stations within a larger launch event.

Somatic Movement

Trauma-informed movement, grounding sequences, gentle mobility, music-led release, and body-awareness exercises that help people reconnect with sensation and safety.

  • •Grounding and arrival movement sequences
  • •Music-led somatic release
  • •Gentle body-awareness practices
  • •Dance and free-form movement
  • •Partner-based trust exercises

Creative Healing

Guided art-making, collage, journaling, expressive prompts, and creative processing for people who need a less verbal doorway into healing.

  • •Mixed-media art workshops
  • •Collage and visual storytelling
  • •Guided journaling and reflection prompts
  • •Music and sound exploration
  • •Group mural and collaborative art

Nervous System Support

Breathwork, sensory regulation tools, quiet room practices, and accessible techniques for managing overwhelm, anxiety, and shutdown states.

  • •Breathwork and vagal toning
  • •Sensory regulation tools and kits
  • •Quiet room and decompression space
  • •Cold/warm sensory reset techniques
  • •Self-compassion and nervous system education

Community Circles

Facilitated group conversations that create space for honesty, peer support, and the kind of connection that happens when people stop performing.

  • •Open storytelling circles
  • •Theme-based discussion groups
  • •Grief, loss, and transition circles
  • •Support for caregivers and parents
  • •Post-experience integration conversations

Youth Programs

Accessible creative healing programs for young people and teens navigating identity, mental health, school stress, and emotional development.

  • •Teen art and expression workshops
  • •Youth emotional literacy circles
  • •Family healing sessions
  • •School-adjacent mental wellness support
  • •Mentorship through creative collaboration

Storytelling & Voices

Story sharing, anonymous reflection, and community narrative work that helps people feel less alone by hearing the truth in someone else's experience.

  • •Anonymous reflection wall and submissions
  • •Live community storytelling sessions
  • •Podcast and voice recordings
  • •Written story archive and publication
  • •Peer testimony events
