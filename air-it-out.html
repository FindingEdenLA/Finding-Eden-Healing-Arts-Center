The stuff people don’t usually say out loud.

Raw, direct, and actually useful

If your brain won’t shut up, if you feel numb and overloaded at the same time, if you already know what’s wrong but still can’t move — start here.

This is not here to talk down to you, hand you a fake affirmation, or treat your pain like a lesson you should already understand. It’s here to actually meet the state you’re in, explain what’s going on underneath it, and give you something real to do with it.

You do not need to be calm to use this. You do not need to be “doing the work” perfectly. You just need to be honest about where you’re at.

Before you start If something here hits too hard, skip ahead, pause, or close the app and come back later. Being honest is useful. Flooding yourself is not.

Quick for when you’re barely holding it together. Deep when you can stay longer. Full when you want the whole thing.
